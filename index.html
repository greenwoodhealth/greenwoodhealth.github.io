Musculoskeletal Expertise






Alongside my women’s health expertise, my NHS and military background has enabled me to develop extensive experience in musculoskeletal physiotherapy, occupational health, and sports injuries. 


My role in the military involved rehabilitating UK Armed Forces athletes to return to peak performance to compete both for inter-services and for Great Britain. 


I have a strong interest in nutrition, hormones, and whole-body health, recognising the important connection between physical and emotional wellbeing.

About me

I am truly passionate about women's health. Our bodies go through so much and too often we are not provided with the information or support to care for them. It is my belief that your body is a temple, and health is the most valuable thing we have. I have struggled with PCOS, pelvic floor dysfunction and pain and for many years didn't know how to help myself, as well as feeling too embarrassed to seek help. This ultimately led me into this job which I love, to help other women, empowering them to live freely and with confidence.
